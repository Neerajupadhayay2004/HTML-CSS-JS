Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pain management services.

What conditions do you treat?

We specialize in treating various chronic pain conditions including fibromyalgia, arthritis, back and neck pain, neuropathic pain, migraines, and other unexplained pain syndromes. Our approach is comprehensive and tailored to each patient's unique needs.

How is your approach different?

Unlike traditional pain management that often focuses solely on symptom relief, we take a whole-person approach. We investigate and address the root causes of your pain, combining advanced medical treatments with lifestyle modifications, nutritional support, and mind-body techniques for comprehensive healing.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers for our medical services. Some of our integrative therapies may not be covered by insurance. Our office staff will verify your benefits and discuss any out-of-pocket costs before beginning treatment.

How long until I see results?

Healing timelines vary depending on your condition, its duration, and your body's response. Some patients experience relief within weeks, while chronic conditions may require several months of treatment. We'll establish realistic expectations during your initial consultation.

What safety measures are in place?

Patient safety is our top priority. All treatments are evidence-based and administered by licensed professionals. We conduct thorough evaluations before recommending any therapy and monitor progress closely to ensure optimal results with minimal risk.
